# Welcome to GerLab

GerLab is a FabLab and creative fabrication space in Mongolia.

We combine digital fabrication, engineering, science, design, and Mongolian maker culture. GerLab is a place where students, researchers, artists, engineers, and makers can learn, prototype, test, fail, rebuild, and share knowledge.

This documentation is made to help GerLab users work safely, use machines correctly, prepare design files, choose materials, and document their projects.

---

## What is GerLab?

GerLab is not only a room with machines.

GerLab is a community workshop where ideas become physical objects. We use modern fabrication tools such as 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, vinyl cutters, electronics tools, and design software.

Our goal is to build a strong maker culture in Mongolia.

## GerLab Culture

GerLab culture is based on making, learning, helping, and documenting.

A good maker does not only create objects. A good maker protects people, respects machines, keeps the workspace clean, teaches others, and leaves useful documentation for the next person.

GerLab connects modern technology with Mongolian creativity, local needs, traditional patterns, nomadic design thinking, and community learning.
